About Us

At Mile High Cuts, we pride ourselves on delivering sharp, modern haircuts and grooming services tailored to every client. Whether you need a clean professional look, a detailed beard trim, or a fresh lineup, our experienced barbers are here to keep you looking your best.

With over 21 years of experience and having served 1500+ satisfied clients, our master barber brings unparalleled expertise to every cut. While we're proud to serve The Colony area, we welcome clients from all over the DFW metroplex to experience our premium grooming services.

Located in the heart of Lewisville, Texas, we've built a reputation for excellence in men's grooming. Our skilled barbers combine traditional techniques with modern trends to give you the perfect cut every time.
